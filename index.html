Hello!

I'm Angelica, a graduate student at Virginia Tech, pursuing a Master of Science in Business Administration with a concentration in Business Analytics.

I have been a Clinical Laboratory Scientist since 2016, largely driven by a desire to make a meaningful impact in this world. As I advanced my career into Laboratory Supervisor and later Laboratory Director, I witnessed firsthand the inefficiencies, poor communication, and lack of analytics in operations. Realizing the potential to make an even greater impact, I pivoted to business analytics to help organizations across industries make smarter, data-driven decisions and solve complex challenges.

I am also a DIY enthusiast who enjoys dabbling in various hobbies, so I created this website as a place to house my side projects, showcase my interests, and document my journey in analytics and beyond.

Thanks for stopping by!
